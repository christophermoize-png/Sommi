Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026

Introduction

Sommi ("we," "our," or "the app") is a wine cellar management application developed by Christopher Moize. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. This is used solely for authentication and displaying your profile within the app.

Wine Collection Data: Information you enter about your wines — names, vintages, ratings, rack assignments, tasting notes, and drinking windows. This data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore and synced across your devices.

Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics. We do not use any third-party analytics services.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Sommi experience:

  • Authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In
  • Store and sync your wine collection across your devices
  • Generate personalized recommendations (Tonight picks, seasonal suggestions, food pairings)
  • Send drinking window alerts and notifications (if enabled)

Data Storage and Security

Your wine collection data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, which provides encryption at rest and in transit. Your data is scoped to your authenticated user ID — no other user can access your collection. Firestore's offline persistence means your data is also cached locally on your device for offline access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your wine collection data is yours alone. We do not serve advertisements and do not share data with advertisers.

Third-Party Services

Sommi uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Authentication: For secure sign-in. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Firestore: For data storage and sync. Data is stored on Google's servers in the United States.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below
  • Export your wine collection data (feature coming soon)

Children's Privacy

Sommi is rated 17+ and is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
